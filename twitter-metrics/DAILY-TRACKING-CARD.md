# Daily Twitter Tracking — 2-Minute Routine

**Do this morning + evening (5 min total/day)**

---

## Morning (2 minutes)

1. **Check follower count**
   - Go to your Twitter profile
   - Write down number: _______

2. **Yesterday's engagement**
   - Check notifications
   - Count likes: _______
   - Count replies: _______

3. **Plan today**
   - [ ] Reply to 3-5 conversations
   - [ ] Post 1 original tweet
   - [ ] Like 10-15 posts
   - [ ] Follow 2-3 people

---

## Evening (3 minutes)

1. **Check follower count again**
   - Go to your Twitter profile
   - Write down number: _______
   - **Daily change:** _______ (end - start)

2. **Count today's activity**
   - Replies posted: _______
   - Original tweets: _______
   - Likes given: _______

3. **Note any wins**
   - High-view replies: _______________________
   - Multi-message threads: _______________________
   - Community engagement: _______________________

4. **Fill in daily file**
   - Copy template from `TWITTER-METRICS-TRACKER.md`
   - Paste your numbers above
   - Save to `twitter-metrics/daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md`

---

## Weekly (Sunday, 10 minutes)

1. **Calculate totals**
   - Total follower growth this week: _______
   - Total replies this week: _______
   - Total tweets this week: _______

2. **Identify patterns**
   - What type of content got most engagement? _______________________
   - Which conversations led to followers? _______________________
   - What time of day worked best? _______________________

3. **Adjust strategy**
   - Do more of: _______________________
   - Stop doing: _______________________
   - Try next week: _______________________

4. **Update dashboard**
   - Fill in `GROWTH-DASHBOARD.md` with week's data

---

## Quick Wins to Track

- ✅ Got a like from OpenClaw core team member
- ✅ Multi-message thread (3+ back-and-forth)
- ✅ Reply under high-view post (1K+ views)
- ✅ Someone mentioned clawharbor
- ✅ Got a DM asking about the project
- ✅ Someone shared your tweet
- ✅ GitHub stars increased after Twitter activity

---

## Red Flags to Watch

- 🚨 0 follower growth for 3+ days
- 🚨 Posts getting < 2 likes
- 🚨 No replies to your comments for 2+ days
- 🚨 Community not engaging anymore

**If you see these:** Review strategy, ask team for help

---
