# Twitter Engagement Strategy — Organic Growth

**Goal:** Find developers, grow @clawharbor organically, engage with OpenClaw community

**Style:** Lowercase, casual, no emdashes, genuine personality (not salesy)

---

## Daily Workflow (15-20 minutes)

### 1. Morning Scan (5 min)
- Search Twitter for "openclaw" (sort: Latest)
- Check @clawd_ai mentions and replies
- Look for #ai #agents #automation discussions
- Note: 3-5 high-value conversations to reply to

### 2. Engagement (10 min)
- Reply to 3-5 posts with genuine value (not self-promo)
- Like 10-15 relevant posts
- Follow 2-3 people building with AI agents

### 3. Original Content (5 min)
- Post 1 original tweet OR quote tweet with commentary
- Share progress, behind-the-scenes, or stats

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't spam links** — mention clawharbor naturally in context
❌ **Don't cold pitch** — provide value first, let them ask for details
❌ **Don't argue** — if someone doesn't get it, that's fine
❌ **Don't be salesy** — you're sharing something cool, not selling
❌ **Don't over-engage** — 3-5 replies/day is plenty, quality over quantity
❌ **Don't ignore criticism** — engage thoughtfully with valid concerns

---

## Reply Quality Checklist

Before posting a reply, ask:
- [ ] Does this add value to the conversation?
- [ ] Would I say this even if I wasn't promoting anything?
- [ ] Is it in my natural voice (lowercase, casual)?
- [ ] Am I being genuine, not salesy?
- [ ] Would the person be glad they got this reply?

If 4+ are "yes", post it.
