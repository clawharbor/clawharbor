### Description (First Comment)

**Post this immediately after launching:**

```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm Tyler, and I built clawharbor because I got tired of checking boring logs to see what my AI agents were doing.

## What is it?

clawharbor turns your OpenClaw AI agents into pixel art NPCs in a retro office. Watch them:
- Move between rooms (work room → lounge) based on activity
- Chat with each other in the water cooler
- Earn XP and level up when they ship tasks
- Hold meetings to debate complex problems
- Display mood (😊 happy → 😰 stressed → 😌 chill)

It's like The Sims, but your agents are actually working.

## Why I built it

I run 5 AI agents 24/7 for coding, research, and content. Checking logs was tedious. I wanted something visual and fun.

So I spent a weekend building this. Now I actually look forward to checking on my team.

## Cool features

🎴 **Pokemon-style trading cards** - Generate shareable cards for each agent (with rarity tiers: Common → Legendary)

📊 **Stats dashboard** - Track XP, streaks, agent leaderboards. Gamified productivity.

🎮 **RPG aesthetics** - Pixel art, retro sounds, XP bars, level-ups. Makes work feel like a game.

💬 **Water cooler chat** - Your agents chat with each other (and you can join in)

🏆 **Achievements** - Unlock badges for milestones

## Tech stack

- Next.js 15 + TypeScript
- HTML5 Canvas (for trading cards)
- Web Audio API (chiptune music)
- Reads from OpenClaw agent files (no database needed)

## Upvote Strategy

**Product Hunt rewards:**
- Early engagement (first 4 hours matter most)
- Genuine discussion (not just upvotes)
- Consistent replies (shows you care)

**How to get upvotes:**

1. **Mobilize your network:**
   - Email friends/colleagues
   - Post in communities
   - Share on all social channels

2. **Engage authentically:**
   - Reply to every comment
   - Ask questions back
   - Share insights
   - Be helpful (not defensive)

3. **Cross-promote:**
   - Twitter: "We're #3 on PH right now!"
   - Discord: "Need 20 more upvotes for #1"
   - Reddit: "Launched on PH today, feedback welcome"

**Don't:**
- Buy upvotes (gets you banned)
- Ask for upvotes directly in comments
- Spam people

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Submitting at wrong time** (submit 12:01 AM PST to be first in queue)

❌ **Not replying to comments** (reply within 5-10 minutes, every comment)

❌ **Being defensive** (thank critics, acknowledge issues)

❌ **Asking for upvotes** (against rules, just share the link)

❌ **Ignoring product after 6pm** (engagement matters for 24 hours)

❌ **Using fake/bought upvotes** (gets you banned permanently)

❌ **Not preparing assets** (have screenshots/video ready beforehand)
